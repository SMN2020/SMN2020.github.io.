Hip Hop Dances

The Running Man

The modern day running man was adopted from the 80s running man. The running man is a dance when you are pretending to run but you stay in place, you also bring your knees up higher than you would when you actually run, all the while pushing your arms out. The 80s running man was performed by people such as MC Hammer and Vanilla Ice. The modern day running man was originally performed by two teenagers from New Jersey. Their names are Kevin Vincent and Jerry Hall. They danced over the song 'My Boo' by Atlantas Ghost Town DJs. This version quickly became viral on the internet, and people began to share videos of themselves doing the dance.

The Whip

Spread your legs slightly.Quickly lift up your right foot, then left. Move up and down, bending your legs a little. Raise your right arm up, with your hand in a thumbs up position facing the left. Now, combine the two!

The Nae Nae

The Nae Nae is a hip-hop dance that involves planting one's feet, swaying with shoulder movement, placing one hand in the air and one hand down, and corporating personal creativity.

Hit The Folks

When you Bend your elbows and lift your leg for a few seconds to match the beat. Bring your arms up as if you are showing your muscles. lift up your leg and lean your back slightly.

The Dab

Dabbing, or the dab, is a simple dance move in which a person drops the head into the bent crook of a slanted arm, typically while raising the opposite arm in a parallel direction but out straight; both arms are pointed to the side and at an upward angle.
